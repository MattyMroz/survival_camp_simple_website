What is Survival Camp?
Survival Camp is a comprehensive training program dedicated to teaching essential survival skills to individuals of all ages. Our experienced instructors provide hands-on training in various survival techniques, including fire-making, shelter-building, navigation, and first aid. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced outdoor enthusiast, our courses are designed to equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to survive in the wilderness.
Who can join the Survival Camp?
Anyone with an interest in learning survival skills can join Survival Camp. We offer courses for all skill levels, from beginners who have never set foot in the wilderness to advanced survivalists looking to hone their skills. Our programs are suitable for individuals, families, and groups, and we welcome participants of all ages. Whether you are looking to build confidence in the outdoors or prepare for an emergency situation, our courses are designed to meet your needs.
What should I bring to the camp?
We recommend bringing comfortable outdoor clothing, sturdy footwear, a water bottle, and any personal items you may need. Specific gear requirements will be provided upon registration, but generally, you should be prepared for various weather conditions and outdoor activities. Essential items include a hat, sunscreen, insect repellent, and a small backpack. For overnight courses, you may need to bring camping gear such as a sleeping bag and tent. Detailed packing lists will be provided for each course.
How long are the courses?
Our courses vary in length from one-day workshops to week-long expeditions. One-day workshops typically focus on specific skills such as fire-making or navigation, while longer courses provide a more comprehensive survival training experience. Week-long expeditions offer an immersive experience where participants can practice and refine their skills in a real-world setting. Detailed information about each course's duration and itinerary can be found on our website.
Are meals provided during the camp?
Yes, meals are provided during the camp. We offer a variety of meal options to accommodate different dietary needs, including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Our meals are designed to be nutritious and energy-boosting, suitable for the physical activities involved in survival training. For overnight courses, we provide all meals, including breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks. Participants are welcome to bring their own snacks and personal food items if they have specific preferences or dietary restrictions.
How do I register for a course?
You can register for a course through our website. Simply select the course you are interested in and follow the registration instructions. Our online registration system is easy to use and allows you to choose your preferred course dates and make secure payments. Once registered, you will receive a confirmation email with detailed information about the course, including what to bring, where to meet, and any additional instructions. If you have any questions or need assistance with the registration process, our customer support team is available to help.
